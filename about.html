My Stitch Story

Irene Marcatante, Founder and Creator of Stitch & B

Hi! My name is Irene, and knitting has always been my favorite type of fiber to consume.

My grandmother was a seamstress, and my mother taught me everything I know. And like so many before us, my daughter's and my crafting night was lovingly coined "Stitching and Bitching."

It was always my hobby until my daughter encouraged me to start posting my creations and sharing them with others after the Pandemic. Knitting has always been a way for women in my family to connect, and I would love to pass on that tradition to new generations. Here's to many more years and people gathering, knitting, and bitchin'.

Love, Irene
